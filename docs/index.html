Welcome – Amr Salem

I work at the intersection of statistical genomics, biomedical informatics, and machine learning. My research includes germline ROH analysis in pediatric cancer, deep learning for kidney stone prediction, polygenic risk scores, and EHR-integrated GWAS. I am currently developing graphtR, an experimental R package that brings Graph Neural Networks to the R ecosystem.

Runs of Homozygosity, statistical genetics, regulatory genomics, rare disease discovery, interpretable AI, and scalable deep learning for biomedicine.

Former Vice President of the Diversity & Inclusion Committee at Case Western. I co-founded a student advocacy group and launched a confidential hotline for sexual harassment reporting.
