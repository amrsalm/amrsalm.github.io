Research

Currently at Dana-Farber Cancer Institute / Harvard Medical School, I'm leading a germline whole-genome analysis project on Runs of Homozygosity (ROH) as markers of tumor suppressor inactivation in pediatric cancer. [arXiv preprint]

At University Hospitals Cleveland Medical Center, I developed CNN-based models to predict kidney stone risk and built scalable pipelines for polygenic risk scores (PRS). [ACMG Poster]

I’m also building graphtR, an experimental R package for Graph Neural Networks in base R, developed in a reading course with Dr. Daniela Calvetti.
